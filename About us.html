❄️Our Story

Bringing Comfort to Your Living Spaces Since 2009

COSY Air Conditioners was founded with a simple mission: to make quality air conditioning accessible to everyone. What started as a small showroom in Mumbai has now grown into one of the leading air conditioning retailers in the region.

We partner with all major brands to bring you the latest technology in cooling solutions. Our team of experts helps you find the perfect AC for your space, budget, and requirements.

With over 15 years of experience in the industry, we have built a reputation for reliability, quality products, and exceptional customer service. Our commitment to customer satisfaction has earned us the trust of thousands of families and businesses across the country
